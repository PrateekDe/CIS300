First‑time flyer essentials

Documents & Check‑in

Passport/ID valid, visas as needed, name matches ticket, and online check‑in 24 hours before departure.

FAA travelers guide →

Packing: 3‑1‑1 liquids

Carry‑on liquids in 3.4oz (100ml) bottles, inside 1 quart‑size bag, 1 bag per traveler. Keep meds and docs in your personal item.

TSA 3‑1‑1 rule →

At the airport

Arrive early, keep ID and boarding pass ready, follow signs for Security and your gate. When in doubt, ask an agent.
