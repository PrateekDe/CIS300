Travel Playbook

Pack smarter, breeze through security, avoid gotchas.

Baggage rules (comparison)

Airline Type Carry-on Checked Bag Notes
US Major 1 cabin bag + personal Often 1×23kg int’l Fees vary by fare
Indian Carrier 1 cabin bag + personal 1–2×23kg int’l Route dependent
Low-cost Small cabin bag Usually add-on Strict size checks

Packing

Roll clothes, use cubes, keep meds + docs in your personal item.

Security

Liquids: 3‑1‑1. Laptops out unless TSA PreCheck. Empty bottle for water.

Connections

Min safe time: 60–90 min domestic → international. Check terminal changes.

Seat selection

Aisle for movement, window for views/rest; exit rows trade legroom for limited recline.

Online check‑in

24 hours before departure; confirm passport info and bag choices to save time.

Boarding groups

Arrive at gate early; listen for groups/priority queues to avoid gate rush.

Personal item must‑haves

ID, meds, chargers, a pen, headphones, snacks, and a light layer.

Medication & docs

Carry prescriptions in original labels; keep copies of ID and itinerary.

Travel insurance

Consider coverage for medical care, delays, and lost baggage—especially abroad.

Money & cards

Notify bank, carry two cards, and have small local cash for taxis/tips.

Phone & roaming

Download airline apps, eSIM or local SIM; set offline maps and boarding passes.

Battery rules

Power banks in carry‑on only; tape exposed terminals; check airline watt‑hour limits.

Food & water

Bring snacks; empty bottle through security, refill after; declare food when required.

Kids & families

Board early if offered; pack activities, wipes, extra clothes, and ear pressure candies.

Pets

Confirm carrier size, health certificates, and fees; arrive early for check‑in.

Special assistance

Request wheelchairs or support in advance; arrive early and inform the gate agent.

Lounge access

Day passes or credit cards may help; great for long connections and showers.

Airport Wi‑Fi & power

Use VPN for sensitive tasks; bring a small power strip or splitter.

Checked‑bag survival

Use a sturdy tag, add a unique ribbon, and AirTag/Tile inside for tracking.

Baggage claim

Note carousel number, verify tag before leaving; report issues immediately.

Delayed or lost bag

File a report at the airline desk; keep receipts for essentials; track with the reference number.

Duty‑free & customs

Liquids bought after security are OK on the same itinerary; keep receipts for connections.

Prohibited items

No blades above limits, flammables, or large lithium batteries in checked bags—check TSA list.

Time zones & jet lag

Shift sleep a day ahead, hydrate, daylight on arrival, and short naps only.
